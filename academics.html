Pre Primary Department

We understand how parents have to put their heart out when they send their child to school. They have high aspirations for their child. At our school, we believe in providing a home-environment to our little learners so that they understand, learn & comprehend the learning provided to them.

In addition we imbibe in our students good culture, respect towards others, good manners & also global values. We reassure that your child grows in the most conducive learning environment.

Curriculum

We emphasize on developing their latent language & maths skills through our specially designed continuous activities.

Key Areas Covered
  • Learning by Exploring & Doing
  • Building developing skills through theme based activities
  • Understanding Language
  • Building Communication skills (English & Hindi)
  • Opportunities for fine and gross motor development
  • Enhancing Mathematical abilities & language understanding
Primary Department

We nurture the dream of setting up an institution of academic excellence. The founder Chairman envisages a quality school system which would strike a perfect balance between Indian culture and tradition and the best western ideals. It would provide technology aided learning to impart the best values on the sound foundations of humanitarianism and integrity.

The institution's mission is to provide a caring learning environment where professional would deal with the learners with exemplary warmth and concern. The mission is also to encourage the learners to develop their individualities and to ensure all round development of the children with the teachers teaming up with the parents. Children are encouraged and guided to 'Dream Big and Think Big'.

Our Deliverables
  • Parental Care for kids
  • Developing Physical, Mental, Logical, Emotional & Spiritual Strength
  • Imbibing the best Manners, Culture, Global Values & Smartness
  • Development of all round Personality & Communication Skills
  • Managed by the most smiling, energetic and affectionate professionals
  • Learning Clubs i.e Media Arts, Robotics, Oratory, Drama, theatre & Life Skills etc.
Facilities
  • Air-conditioned Classrooms
  • Well-equipped library
  • Safe cold drinking water treated with RO
  • CC TV installed throughout with continuous monitoring
  • Facilities for Yoga, Karate, Dance, Music, Art-craft, etc.
  • Separate & Dedicated infrastructure for students, that is Intellectual development rooms, Performing Arts room, Computer labs etc.
Curriculum

Affiliated to CBSE, with classes from Nursery to XII. CBSE curriculum is followed.

Senior Department

"EXCELLENCE THROUGH CONTINUOUS LEARNING"

We understand that multifariousness education options can create confusions among students and they face problems in selecting the best career option.

At our school, we provide career counselling to our students to select & pursue the best option available to them in light of their own strengths & weaknesses.

We make them understand the importance of these years in his life and also continuously follow their progress through our expert evaluative systems. We are known for our strictness "star review planning for our students."
