About School

Sulekh Convent Public School was formally established with a vision to provide high quality educational environment giving its learners a cutting edge with 3 A's — Ability, Achievement and Appreciation — which goes hand-in-hand meeting the on-going demands of the world over. We prepare leaders for future with an insightful vision and a compassionate sensibility.

Vision

Our vision is focussed to create a school community where children participate, excel and are proud of their achievements. We aspire to provide our pupils the best & the latest information to update themselves, to find themselves at par with this competitive world.

Each day we provide a balanced approach towards learning, giving the parents peace of mind that their child is in safe hands. We recognize that curriculum is more than what appears in manuals or a series of activities. We believe curriculum is everything that happens in the classroom from interaction and meals to singing and playing. We never miss an opportunity to educate our students. We develop in children the life skills that go beyond reading, writing and mathematics.

School Policies
Quality Policy

The school shall constantly strive to provide an environment that stimulates student learning by consistently using relevant curriculum and scientific teaching methods that promotes academic excellence.

The School shall adopt new and innovative teaching methods and technological aids to enhance teaching-learning processes. The school shall encourage and enable teachers to upgrade their skills and knowledge in continuous quest to advance academic standards of the school.

The basis of learning shall be founded on the values that enable every student to become a happy, healthy and productive member of our society.

Talent Development Policy

The school shall strive to identify the gifts and talents of diverse learners and provide opportunities for individual competencies to develop their full potential. The school shall engage with guardians and parents to ensure that identified gifts and talents are nurtured both within and outside the school environment.

Safety Policy

The school is committed to establish and maintain a safe and a healthy working environment throughout the school by adopting safe working procedures and promoting these among the staff and students of the school.

The school shall promote a culture of safety by constantly reinforcing these practices through training and awareness so that each student and staff is a safety ambassador in the community.

Environment Policy

The school is committed to contributing to environmental preservation by promoting the value of earth consciousness and adopting reduce, reuse and recycle processes in all school activities refuse, reduce, reuse, recycle and rejoice.

Nanhe Nagrik Policy

The school shall strive to inculcate value of compassion for all creations, love for the nation and respect for all individuals so that students and staff find joy in serving humanity and are recognised as exemplary citizens of the world.

Philosophy

The school strives to be the region's trusted and well regarded private education institution and this can be attained through sensitive management, professional ethical, courteous and knowledgeable staff.

We consider our employees the people who give us their skills, energy and time to be our greatest asset.

We have a responsibility to assure our employees a decent, progressive standard of living, safe working conditions and a fair system of material rewards for their work, at least as favorable as those provided by other leading companies operations.

The School also recognises an obligation to provide employees with opportunities to acquire and develop their skills through training in order to qualify for positions of increasing responsibilities and to gain personal satisfaction through achieving the most from their abilities.

Salient Feature
Radio Frequency Identification Detection (RFID)

Keeping in mind, the safety of the students, we have initiated a unique RFID system to track the students entry and exit from the school. This automatic attendance system of recording provides the information through messages to their phones on daily basis. It also includes other significant messages through the SMS's.

RFID Image
E-Connect Portal

E-Connect service is the communication portal which helps in bridging the gap between the parents and teachers and reduces response time to parent's queries. E-Connect portal is easy to access and simple to use which enables parents to communicate with the teachers about their ward(s) information comprising transport details, time table, syllabus etc.

I-Exam

Continuous and Comprehensive Evaluation allows the facilitator to observe and help identify the areas of aptitude and the attitude of the child over a period of time. This allows the evaluator to recognize patterns instead of stand alone examination for evaluators.

Specialised Faculty for Performing Arts, Sports and Clubs

We have specialised faculty for P.A. that includes dance, theatre, vocal, drums, guitar, synthesizer etc to develop aesthetic skills of the students. The school has sports facilities like cricket pitch (concrete and grass), football field, volleyball, table tennis, skating, chess and carrom, running track, taekwondo, kho-kho and yoga. On Wednesdays, the school has special clubs like Fashion, Robotics, Knowledge Bowl, Oratory, Creative Writing, Calligraphy, Food Factory, Clay Moulding, Culinary Arts, Media Arts etc.

Sports Image
Multi Safety Security System

The school has CCTV cameras at each and every nook and corner of the school premises for the safety and security of the child. In the buses the school provides RFID system of attendance to ensure the safety of each child. The school has security guards at the entrance and exit gates who ensure that safety is the top priority.

Security Image
Learning Support System (Counsellors and Special Educators)

The school has appointed special educators and counsellors to take care of special children where the individual attention is given to them. The counsellors also counsel the students from time to time to solve their issues by providing guidance to them.

Events and Activities

The school conducts many events and activities throughout the year such as FITNESSOR and SAFE AND GREEN INITIATIVE like MARATHON etc. to encourage the importance of healthy living and cleanliness in the lives of the students. The school also organises ANNUAL DAY, SPORTS DAY, SCIENCE EXHIBITION etc. to develop the co-scholastic skills of the students.
