Admission Guidelines

For completing the admission formalities, parents are requested to fill the admission form, transport form (where applicable) and submit to the school office along with mandatory documents given below:

Class III to V

Birth Certificate, previous school report card and Original TC is required from previous school for admission to Class-III and above. Admission will not be granted in case TC is not submitted.

Class VI Onwards

Birth Certificate, previous school report card and Original TC duly counter signed by the respective board. Online application form is available on the school website.

Transfer / Withdrawal

It becomes necessary sometimes for the parents to withdraw their child from the school due to family re-location. In such cases parents need to follow certain steps for a hustle free procedure.

  • Withdrawal notice by other means such as email or verbal notice will not be accepted
  • For getting transfer certificates and character certificates etc., the parents have to give a written application
  • No student will be permitted to withdraw after the commencement of the session i.e. after 1st April unless fee upto fee end of summer vacation is paid
NOTE

'VPS Withdrawal Policy' is fair and reasonable that allows the students to withdraw from the school, as well as for the school to expel students in accordance with the Expulsion Policy & Fee payment Policy.

Fee Structure
FEE

The total fee/ charges are to be paid in four installments i.e., quarterly.

QuarterMonthLast Date
IApril – JuneApril 20
IIJuly – SeptemberJuly 20
IIIOctober – DecemberOctober 20
IVJanuary – MarchJanuary 20
  • The mode of payment is Cheque/DD only, in favour of the school
  • Post dated cheques will not be accepted
  • School office will remain open during summer and winter break
  • Fine of ₹20 per day will be charged on the late payment after due date
  • If the fee/charges are not paid by the end of the quarter the defaulter's name shall be struck off the school rolls
  • Parents may keep the fee deposit receipts in safe custody and produce these as proof of their payments
  • Fee once paid shall not be refunded under any circumstances
  • There will be nominal increase in school fees & other miscellaneous charges every year
