Clubs

At our school, we provide an opportunity to every student to develop and realise his/her passion through continuous learning and experiential application. With this aim, we have developed the following groups:

Group A

This Group consists of the various Clubs to expose the students towards their non-academic interest and provide them a platform to develop these interests and show their performances. The groups will be work in line with their academic subjects so that students can gain maximum in School environment.

Group B

This Group consists of various performing Arts disciplines, whereby the students may select their area of interest and get a chance to present their talent in front of large audiences. The students would be taught as per their age requirements and the designed curriculum will help them to bring out the best in them.

Group C

This Group consists of sports activities so that students remain physically fit and understand the importance of exercise and play in the busy schedules of their academic output.

House Systems
At our school, we have four houses namely
  • Adinath House
  • Saurabh House
  • Parshvanath House
  • Mahavir House

Students are divided among these houses so that they get equal opportunities to exhibit their talents and inner capabilities and learn to work in teams.

Role of Houses is Limited to the Following
  • Maintenance of Overall School Discipline
  • Coordination of competitions as per the scheduled structure mentioned in the Almanac

**Monthly House meets will be organized to assign house duties & house duties will be assigned on weekly basis.

D.E.A.R — Drop Everything & Read

"Drop Everything & Read" — This is an innovative method proposed at our School to inculcate the habit of reading among our students that will make them more expressive and communicative in the society.

Students are given one dedicated period for this, where they leave every work and only read, read & read!!!

Discipline Card System

As punctuality and discipline are integral part of the school system so school follows a card system as a monitoring tool for discipline. To ensure proper discipline in school the following card system will been forced:

🟡 Yellow Card: Absenteeism, Uniform & Late Coming

To be issued to the students defaulter for the following:

  • Remaining absent from the school for three or more consecutive days without intimation
  • Not being in proper school uniform or without ID Card even after continuous reminders
  • Non submission of leave application immediately after joining back

🟠 Orange Card: Academics

  • Repeated careless and irregular work
  • Not bringing the Almanac
  • Using unfair means during examination
  • Non completion of homework without any genuine reason
  • Not bringing the books & copies as per the scheduled time table
  • Missing ET's/UT's/ET's

🔴 Red Card: Discipline

  • Misbehaving with a teacher
  • Disturbing classroom teaching
  • Loitering out of the class
  • Using abusive language
  • Being disobedient and aggressive
  • Bullying fellow students and juniors
  • Damaging school property
  • Use of electronic gadgets

⚫ Black Card

To be issued by the Principal. Issue of black card will entail suspension for at least 30 days or expulsion from the school with immediate effect depending on the gravity of the offence.

Negative Influence
  • Yellow Card: Not allowed to go out in the break on that day or for a week
  • Orange Card: Prohibition from the co-curricular activities
  • Red Card: Suspension from the school for 3 to 7 days depending on the gravity of the offence
